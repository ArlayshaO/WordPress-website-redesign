Who We Are

Since the early 90s, the Firefighters Community Service Organization of North Kansas City has served and educated the local metro area where we work and live.

Armed with a network of volunteers and gracious contributions from residents, our charitable community organization in Missouri is devoted to improving the quality of life for families and individuals.

Our Story

This Community Service Organization serves to improve the community of North Kansas City. We are a group of firefighters who serve those in need.

There is more than one way to help.

Your monetary donations go towards lumber for leaky roofs, smoke and carbon monoxide detectors to protect a family while they sleep, child seats for working parents, food and clothing for the naked and hungry, and so much more. All donations are tax deductible.
