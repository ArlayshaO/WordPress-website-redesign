Exciting Educational Events

Together with local churches and organizations in Kansas City, Missouri, we organize educational events for schools and public gatherings.

The Fire Fighters Community Service Organization of North Kansas City has previously worked with North Kansas City High School to whom prom attire was collected using a clothes closet. Moreover, our group donates equipment to athletic departments for after-school programs.
